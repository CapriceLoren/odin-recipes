Brandon's Puttanesca

Lets talk about my boyfriend's puttanesca recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 28 oz can diced tomato
  • 4 cloves garlic, dices
  • 4 fillets anchovy
  • 1/4 cup capers
  • 1/2 cup kalamata olives, roughly chopped
  • 1 tsp crushed red pepper
  • caper and olive juice, to taste
  • 1 lb fettuccine

Steps

  1. Cook pasta to package instructions
  2. Heat oil on medium in large, deep skillet
  3. When oil is hot, add garlic and anchovy
  4. Cook until garlic is lightly browned and anchovy is dissolved
  5. Add tomatos and red pepper
  6. Add capers and olives
  7. Simmer for about twenty minutes, adding caper and olive juices to taste
  8. Salt to taste, and serve!
