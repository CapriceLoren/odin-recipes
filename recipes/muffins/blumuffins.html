Blueberry Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups blueberries

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F
  2. Combine dry ingredients into bowl, mix well
  3. Cream together butter and sugar
  4. Add eggs 1 at a time until well incorporated
  5. Add vanilla
  6. Add dry ingredients to creamed butter and sugar alternatively with milk
  7. Crush 1/2 cup of blueberries, mix into batter. Fold in the rest.
  8. Add batter to tins, sprinkle sugar on top.
  9. Bake for 30-35 minutes
