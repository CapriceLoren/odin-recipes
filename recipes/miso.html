Miso Soup

Lets talk about miso soup

Ingredients

  • 1/4 block of Silken tofu cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tbsp dashi
  • 1/2 an onion, sliced
  • Dried nori, cut into thin strips
  • 3 tbsp miso paste

Steps

  1. Add water, onion, dashi, and seaweed to a pot.
  2. Bring water to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat. Let everything simmer until onions are tender, about 10 minutes.
  4. Turn off heat, add miso paste
  5. Serve and enjoy!
